# Account Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Purpose

This Account Deletion Policy explains how users of Kasi Room Network (KRN) may request deletion of their accounts and how related information is handled after deletion.

---

## 2. User-Initiated Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account at any time.

Account deletion requests may be submitted through:

- The account settings page (when available).
- A support request.
- Email communication with KRN support.

KRN may require verification of account ownership before processing deletion requests.

---

## 3. What Happens When an Account Is Deleted

When an account is deleted:

- Access to the account is removed.
- Active login sessions may be terminated.
- Public profile information is removed from the platform.
- Listings associated with the account may be removed or deactivated.
- Future use of platform services may require creation of a new account.

---

## 4. Information That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained after account deletion where necessary for:

- Legal compliance.
- Fraud prevention.
- Security investigations.
- Audit requirements.
- Dispute resolution.
- Enforcement of platform policies.

Examples may include:

- Transaction records.
- Verification records.
- Moderation actions.
- Security logs.
- Communication records related to abuse reports.

Retention periods are governed by KRN's Data Retention Policy.

---

## 5. Listings and User Content

Upon deletion:

- Listings may be removed from public view.
- Uploaded images may be deleted or anonymized.
- User-generated content may be retained where required for legal, operational, or security purposes.
- Reports involving the deleted account may remain in administrative records.

---

## 6. Messaging Data

Messages exchanged with other users may not be immediately deleted because:

- Other participants have legitimate access to conversation history.
- Records may be required for abuse investigations.
- Security and moderation obligations may require temporary retention.

Where possible, deleted accounts may be anonymized within historical conversations.

---

## 7. Verification Records

Landlord verification records, moderation records, fraud reports, and related administrative data may be retained after account deletion where necessary to:

- Prevent repeat abuse.
- Support future investigations.
- Meet legal obligations.

---

## 8. Processing Time

KRN will aim to process verified deletion requests within a reasonable period.

Additional time may be required where:

- Identity verification is pending.
- Active investigations are underway.
- Legal obligations require delayed deletion.

---

## 9. Account Recovery

Once deletion has been completed, account recovery may not be possible.

Users are encouraged to ensure they have retained any information they may need before requesting deletion.

---

## 10. Refusal or Delay of Deletion Requests

KRN may refuse or delay deletion requests where:

- Required by law.
- Necessary for fraud investigations.
- Necessary for security investigations.
- Required to resolve disputes.
- Required to protect the rights of other users.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

KRN may update this policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be published on the platform with a revised effective date.

Continued use of KRN after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
