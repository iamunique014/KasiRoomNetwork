# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Purpose

Kasi Room Network (KRN) is a platform that connects tenants and landlords to find and advertise rental rooms and accommodation opportunities.

This Acceptable Use Policy explains what users may and may not do when using the platform.

By using KRN, you agree to follow this policy.

---

## 2. Permitted Use

You may use KRN to:

- Search for rooms and accommodation.
- Create and manage rental listings.
- Contact landlords or tenants regarding accommodation.
- Manage your user profile.
- Use platform features as intended.

All information provided must be accurate and truthful.

---

## 3. Prohibited Activities

Users may not:

### 3.1 False or Misleading Listings

- Create fake listings.
- Advertise rooms that do not exist.
- Upload misleading photos.
- Provide false pricing information.
- Misrepresent room availability.

### 3.2 Fraudulent Activity

- Attempt scams or financial fraud.
- Request payments for non-existent accommodation.
- Impersonate another person or organization.
- Use stolen identities or documents.

### 3.3 Spam and Abuse

- Send unsolicited promotional messages.
- Repeatedly contact users without legitimate purpose.
- Use automated tools to send bulk messages.
- Create multiple accounts to bypass restrictions.

### 3.4 Harmful Content

Users may not upload or share content that:

- Is illegal.
- Contains threats or harassment.
- Promotes violence.
- Contains hate speech.
- Contains discriminatory language.
- Violates another person's rights.

### 3.5 Platform Abuse

Users may not:

- Attempt unauthorized access to systems.
- Reverse engineer platform functionality.
- Interfere with platform operations.
- Upload malicious software.
- Attempt denial-of-service attacks.
- Circumvent security controls.

---

## 4. Listing Requirements

Landlords must ensure:

- Listings accurately represent the accommodation.
- Photos reflect the actual property or room.
- Prices are clearly stated.
- Availability information is current.
- Contact information is accurate.

KRN may remove listings that fail to meet these requirements.

---

## 5. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- Maintaining account security.
- Protecting login credentials.
- Ensuring information remains accurate.
- Complying with applicable laws.
- Respecting other users.

Users remain responsible for activity performed through their accounts.

---

## 6. Reporting Violations

Users may report:

- Fake listings.
- Fraud attempts.
- Harassment.
- Spam.
- Misleading information.
- Safety concerns.

Reports may be reviewed by administrators and moderators.

---

## 7. Enforcement Actions

KRN may take action against users who violate this policy, including:

- Content removal.
- Listing suspension.
- Listing removal.
- Account warnings.
- Temporary account suspension.
- Permanent account termination.
- Reporting unlawful conduct to relevant authorities.

Enforcement decisions are made at KRN's discretion.

---

## 8. No Guarantee of Verification

While KRN may perform listing and landlord verification processes, users remain responsible for conducting their own due diligence before entering into rental agreements or making payments.

Verification does not constitute a guarantee of legitimacy or suitability.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

KRN may update this policy at any time.

Updated versions will be published on the platform with a revised effective date.

Continued use of KRN after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
