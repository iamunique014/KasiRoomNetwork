# Reporting and Appeals Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Purpose

Kasi Room Network (KRN) is committed to maintaining a safe, trustworthy, and reliable platform for landlords, tenants, and other users.

This Reporting and Appeals Policy explains:

- How users can report violations.
- How reports are reviewed.
- Possible enforcement actions.
- How users may appeal certain decisions.

---

## 2. What Can Be Reported

Users may report content, listings, accounts, or activity that may violate KRN policies.

Examples include:

### 2.1 Fraudulent Listings

- Fake room listings.
- Non-existent properties.
- Misleading photos.
- False pricing information.
- False availability claims.

### 2.2 Spam

- Repeated unsolicited messages.
- Promotional content unrelated to accommodation.
- Bulk messaging activity.
- Fake account activity.

### 2.3 Harassment and Abuse

- Threatening behavior.
- Harassment.
- Intimidation.
- Offensive conduct.
- Hate speech.

### 2.4 Safety Concerns

- Suspicious activity.
- Potential scams.
- Dangerous accommodation conditions.
- Identity misuse.

### 2.5 Policy Violations

- Violations of the Acceptable Use Policy.
- Circumvention of platform restrictions.
- Attempts to manipulate platform systems.

---

## 3. How to Submit a Report

Reports may be submitted through:

- Report buttons within the platform.
- Listing reporting tools.
- User profile reporting tools.
- Customer support channels.
- Email communication with KRN support.

Reports should include as much relevant information as possible.

Examples:

- Listing details.
- User information.
- Screenshots.
- Message history.
- Description of the concern.

---

## 4. Review Process

When a report is received, KRN may:

1. Review submitted information.
2. Examine related platform activity.
3. Review messages, listings, or profiles involved.
4. Request additional information.
5. Determine whether a policy violation occurred.

Not every report will result in enforcement action.

---

## 5. Investigation Standards

KRN may consider:

- Evidence provided by users.
- Platform records.
- Verification status.
- Prior enforcement history.
- Severity of the alleged violation.
- Potential risk to other users.

Investigations may be automated, manual, or both.

---

## 6. Possible Enforcement Actions

Where violations are identified, KRN may take one or more actions:

### Content Actions

- Remove photos.
- Remove listings.
- Remove profile content.
- Restrict visibility of content.

### Account Actions

- Warning notices.
- Temporary restrictions.
- Temporary suspension.
- Permanent suspension.
- Account termination.

### Verification Actions

- Removal of verification status.
- Suspension of verification privileges.
- Additional verification requirements.

### Safety Actions

- Limiting platform functionality.
- Blocking communication features.
- Escalating serious matters to relevant authorities where legally required.

---

## 7. False or Abusive Reports

Users may not intentionally submit:

- False reports.
- Malicious reports.
- Harassing reports.
- Repeated reports intended to abuse moderation systems.

Abuse of reporting tools may result in enforcement action.

---

## 8. Appeals

Users may appeal certain moderation or enforcement decisions.

Examples include:

- Listing removals.
- Account suspensions.
- Verification decisions.
- Content removals.

Not all decisions may be eligible for appeal.

---

## 9. Appeal Submission

Appeals should include:

- The decision being appealed.
- Relevant account information.
- Any supporting evidence.
- An explanation of why the user believes the decision was incorrect.

Appeals may be submitted through support channels designated by KRN.

---

## 10. Appeal Review

Appeals may be reviewed by:

- Platform administrators.
- Moderation personnel.
- Authorized reviewers.

The appeal review may consider:

- Original evidence.
- Additional evidence.
- Platform records.
- Applicable policies.

A prior decision may be:

- Upheld.
- Modified.
- Reversed.

---

## 11. Final Decisions

After review, KRN may issue a final determination.

Repeated appeals regarding the same matter may not be reviewed unless substantial new information is provided.

---

## 12. Response Times

Investigation and appeal response times may vary depending on:

- Complexity of the matter.
- Volume of reports.
- Availability of evidence.
- Security or legal considerations.

KRN does not guarantee specific response times.

---

## 13. User Safety

Users should contact local emergency services or relevant authorities if they believe there is an immediate threat to personal safety.

KRN is an accommodation platform and is not an emergency response service.

---

## 14. Policy Updates

KRN may update this policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be published with a revised effective date.

Continued use of KRN after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
