# Data Retention Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Purpose

This Data Retention Policy explains how Kasi Room Network (KRN) stores, retains, archives, and deletes user information.

The purpose of retention is to:

- Operate the platform effectively.
- Maintain security.
- Prevent fraud and abuse.
- Meet legal and regulatory obligations.
- Support dispute resolution and investigations.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to information collected through:

- User accounts.
- Profiles.
- Listings.
- Photos.
- Messages.
- Verification processes.
- Reports and complaints.
- Platform usage logs.

---

## 3. Retention Principles

KRN aims to:

- Retain information only as long as necessary.
- Protect retained information using reasonable security measures.
- Remove or anonymize information when retention is no longer required.
- Comply with applicable legal obligations.

---

## 4. Account Information

Examples:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile information
- Account settings

Retention Period:

- Active account duration.
- Up to 24 months after account deletion unless a longer period is required for legal, fraud-prevention, or security purposes.

---

## 5. Listings and Property Information

Examples:

- Room listings
- Property details
- Pricing information
- Listing descriptions
- Listing photos

Retention Period:

- While listings remain active.
- Archived for up to 24 months after removal or account deletion.
- Longer retention may occur where required for fraud investigations or dispute resolution.

---

## 6. Uploaded Photos

Examples:

- Property photos
- Room photos
- Verification images

Retention Period:

- While associated listings remain active.
- Archived for up to 24 months after listing removal.
- Deleted or anonymized thereafter unless required for investigations or legal compliance.

---

## 7. Messaging Data

Examples:

- Conversations
- Messages exchanged between users

Retention Period:

- While accounts remain active.
- Up to 24 months after account deletion.
- Longer retention may occur where messages are relevant to abuse reports, fraud investigations, or legal obligations.

---

## 8. Verification Records

Examples:

- Landlord verification status
- Verification notes
- Administrative review actions
- Verification audit logs

Retention Period:

- Up to 7 years after verification activity.
- Longer where required by law or ongoing investigations.

---

## 9. Reports, Complaints, and Moderation Records

Examples:

- User reports
- Appeals
- Investigation notes
- Enforcement actions
- Spam reports

Retention Period:

- Up to 7 years after resolution.

These records may be retained longer where necessary to protect users, prevent repeat abuse, or comply with legal requirements.

---

## 10. Security Logs

Examples:

- Login attempts
- IP addresses
- Device information
- Access logs
- Security alerts

Retention Period:

- Up to 24 months.

Security logs may be retained longer when associated with active investigations.

---

## 11. Analytics and Operational Data

Examples:

- Usage statistics
- Performance metrics
- Error logs
- System monitoring information

Retention Period:

- Typically up to 24 months.
- May be anonymized and retained longer for business analysis.

---

## 12. Legal Retention Requirements

KRN may retain information longer than the periods described in this policy when necessary to:

- Comply with applicable laws.
- Respond to lawful requests.
- Resolve disputes.
- Protect platform security.
- Prevent fraud and abuse.
- Enforce platform policies.

---

## 13. Data Deletion and Anonymization

When retention is no longer required, KRN may:

- Permanently delete information.
- Aggregate information.
- Anonymize information so that it can no longer identify an individual user.

---

## 14. User Requests

Users may request:

- Access to personal information.
- Correction of inaccurate information.
- Deletion of eligible information.

Such requests are subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

---

## 15. Policy Updates

KRN may update this policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be published on the platform with a revised effective date.

Continued use of KRN after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
