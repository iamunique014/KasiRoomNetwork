# Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## Welcome

Kasi Room Network (KRN) exists to help people find accommodation safely, efficiently, and respectfully.

These Community Guidelines explain the standards expected from everyone using the platform.

Our goal is to create a trusted environment where landlords and tenants can connect with confidence.

---

## 1. Be Honest

Trust is the foundation of the platform.

Users should:

- Provide accurate information.
- Use real contact details.
- Upload genuine photos.
- Clearly describe rooms and properties.
- Keep pricing and availability up to date.

Do not:

- Create fake listings.
- Use misleading photos.
- Pretend to be another person.
- Misrepresent a property or room.

---

## 2. Treat Others With Respect

Everyone deserves to be treated respectfully.

Users should:

- Communicate professionally.
- Remain courteous during discussions.
- Respect differences and opinions.
- Resolve disagreements constructively.

Do not:

- Harass others.
- Threaten users.
- Use abusive language.
- Engage in bullying or intimidation.

---

## 3. Keep Communication Relevant

KRN messaging tools are intended for accommodation-related communication.

Appropriate examples include:

- Room availability questions.
- Rental pricing discussions.
- Viewing arrangements.
- Property information requests.

Do not use messaging for:

- Spam.
- Unrelated promotions.
- Chain messages.
- Repeated unwanted contact.

---

## 4. Protect Your Safety

Always exercise caution when interacting with other users.

Recommended practices:

- Verify information independently.
- Inspect accommodation before making commitments.
- Keep records of important conversations.
- Meet in safe locations when possible.
- Trust your instincts if something seems suspicious.

If you feel unsafe, stop communication and report the issue.

---

## 5. Protect Your Personal Information

Avoid sharing unnecessary personal information.

Examples include:

- Banking passwords.
- Identity numbers.
- Security codes.
- Sensitive financial information.

Only share information necessary to complete legitimate accommodation arrangements.

---

## 6. No Fraud or Scams

Fraudulent activity is strictly prohibited.

Examples include:

- Fake listings.
- Fake payments.
- Identity fraud.
- False verification claims.
- Deposit scams.
- Misleading accommodation offers.

Accounts involved in fraudulent activity may be permanently removed.

---

## 7. Fair Housing and Non-Discrimination

Users should treat all individuals fairly and respectfully.

Do not use the platform to:

- Harass individuals based on personal characteristics.
- Promote hate or discrimination.
- Exclude people unlawfully.

Users are expected to comply with applicable South African laws and regulations.

---

## 8. Report Problems

Help keep the community safe by reporting:

- Fake listings.
- Scam attempts.
- Spam.
- Harassment.
- Suspicious activity.
- Safety concerns.

Timely reporting helps protect other users.

---

## 9. Verification Is Not a Guarantee

KRN may verify:

- Listings.
- Properties.
- Landlords.

Verification improves trust but does not guarantee:

- Property quality.
- User honesty.
- Rental outcomes.
- Suitability of accommodation.

Users should always perform their own checks.

---

## 10. Respect Platform Rules

Users are expected to comply with:

- Terms and Conditions.
- Privacy Policy.
- Acceptable Use Policy.
- Reporting and Appeals Policy.
- Other published KRN policies.

Repeated violations may result in restrictions or account removal.

---

## 11. Building a Trusted Community

Every user contributes to the quality of the platform.

A strong KRN community is built on:

- Honesty.
- Respect.
- Accountability.
- Safety.
- Transparency.

By following these guidelines, users help create a platform that benefits both landlords and tenants.

---

## 12. Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

- Warnings.
- Content removal.
- Listing removal.
- Verification removal.
- Temporary suspension.
- Permanent account termination.

Serious violations may be referred to relevant authorities where required.

---

## 13. Changes to These Guidelines

KRN may update these Community Guidelines from time to time.

Updated versions will be published with a revised effective date.

Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised guidelines.
